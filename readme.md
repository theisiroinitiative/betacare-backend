# BetaCare — Your Friendly WhatsApp Health Companion 🇳🇬

Welcome to **BetaCare**! We help Nigerians stay on top of their health and medical history using a tool almost everyone already knows how to use: **WhatsApp**.

Many people find modern medical apps or websites confusing to navigate, or simply don't have the time to learn how to use them. BetaCare solves this by bringing a smart, caring assistant directly into your WhatsApp chat, while keeping your information organized and secure in the background.

---

## 🌟 How BetaCare Works

BetaCare operates in two simple spaces:
1. **A Simple Web Interface**: Used just for your initial sign-up, setting up your core health details, and giving your doctor permission to see your records.
2. **WhatsApp Bot**: Where you spend 99% of your time interacting, asking questions, logging how you feel, and receiving reminders.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. Simple Onboarding & Verification
Setting up your account is a breeze:
- **Stage 1 (Account Setup)**: Enter your name, email, and phone number on our web portal and verify it with a quick email code.
- **Stage 2 (Health Profile)**: Enter some basic health details (like date of birth, blood group, genotype, current conditions, medications, and emergency contacts).
- **Activate WhatsApp**: Once your profile is ready, a special code is sent to you. Send this code to the BetaCare WhatsApp number, and your chat link is immediately activated!

### 2. Friendly Pidgin-Speaking AI Companion
Your main interaction is with our WhatsApp AI Bot, which is custom-built to feel like a warm, supportive Nigerian companion:
- **Speaks Pidgin**: The bot talks to you in friendly, everyday Pidgin English so everything feels natural and easy.
- **Always Aware**: It remembers your health conditions, medications, and recent history, so you never have to repeat yourself.
- **Listen to Complaints**: If you have symptom complaints (e.g., *"My head dey pain me"* or *"I dey feel warm"*), it listens, notes down how severe it is, and saves it in your file.
- **Logs Vitals**: You can tell the bot your vitals (e.g., *"My blood sugar na 110"* or *"I just check my blood pressure, e dey 120 over 80"*), and it updates your health log.
- **Asks About Medication**: It follows up to verify you're taking your pills on schedule.

> ⚠️ **What the AI Bot Cannot Do (Strict Safety Rules)**
> To keep you safe, the AI bot **never** makes a medical diagnosis, **never** prescribes drugs, and **never** gives professional medical advice. If you ask it for treatment advice, it will politely advise you to see your doctor.

### 3. Smart Reminders & Alarms
Never forget your pills, exercises, or checkups again:
- The AI can automatically set up reminders based on what you tell it (e.g., *"Remind me to take my medicine at 8 PM daily"*).
- The scheduler runs in the background and sends a direct WhatsApp message to your phone the exact minute a reminder is due.
- It supports daily, weekly, or monthly repeating reminders.

### 4. Secure Hospital & Doctor Integration
When you visit a clinic or hospital, BetaCare makes it easy for doctors to give you better care:
- **Multi-Department Setup**: Hospitals can onboard their various departments (ENT, Pediatrics, etc.) and practitioners.
- **Doctor Linkage**: You choose exactly which doctors can see your medical records by linking them via your profile.
- **Practitioner Portal**: Linked doctors can securely log in to their dashboard, search for you by your phone number, view your medical history, add new diagnosed conditions, and prescribe medications.
- **Strict Privacy**: A doctor **cannot** see your profile or details unless you have linked them to your account. You can unlink a doctor at any time to immediately revoke their access.

---

## 🔒 Security & Data Privacy
We understand that your health information is deeply personal. BetaCare secures your profile with advanced encryption and requires password/token validation at every step. Your WhatsApp chat identifier is linked uniquely to your account, ensuring no one else can view or modify your data.
